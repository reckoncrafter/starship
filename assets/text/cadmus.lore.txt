WEISS MINING CORPORATION: CADMUS LOCATION

Little is known about Weiss' operations on CADMUS.

According to public corporation records, surveyors
were sent to evaluate the asteroid belt for the supected
presence of large deposits of elemental Neodymium.

The record states that the survey team made it to the
star system, and contact was lost shortly after.

On 2711, the Weiss survey team missed their expected
return date.
